examples:

A static website is just that - static. It is not dynamic. It does not change (frequently).

An example might be a Restaurant's menu, but without order processing.

text - anything written in plain English, or whatever language.

images/logos/videos

just about anything seen on the internet can be expressed as a single, static web page.

how it works:

You explain to me your ideas during a "meeting".

Think: word content, images, color schemes, etc.

I do my best to implement those ideas over the next few days.

Completely free of charge.



When it comes time to make the site 'Live', we work together to find a low-cost hosting solution. At no point do you pay me. In most cases, there will be a fee for hosting.

The idea is to add a very low-cost entry point to being on the internet.

You don't pay a dev, you pay for hosting the site. Both of these range in price anywhere from free to cheap to expensive, depending on the content.
